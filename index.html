" The difficulty lies not so much in developing new ideas as in escaping from old ones. "

-John Maynard Keynes

e are building Internet products for our clients since 1999. We know how to do that very well. We can deliver complex software projects on time. And we can do this repeatedly. We are a bunch of hackers / programmers, a very special kind of knowledge workers. And we know which tools we need. We already have a bunch of tools built for ourselves. And now we want to bring these tools - and stuff we think is still missing to work efficiently together in groups over the internet - to the rest of the world. We want to build tools for knowledge workers. We think the co-working idea great. We love to build things. And we have the financial resources to do so. With DeadPresidents-Investments we are investing only in our own ideas.

We are not planning to build companies to sell them: We want to build great companies with outstanding products because it is so much fun.

And if you buildsomething great and have fun doing it, we are sure that we will make money, too. So our vision is: Having fun working with great. people. and making money because our customers really like our products. It is really that simple.
